Welcome to Plant Power.

In this game you'll learn about what plants need to grow. You'll be responsible for growing this special flower we have.

Make sure to give it enough water. You can see how much water it has on the right side of your screen - don't let it run out!

Make sure the plant has enough light as well.

Can you get it to reach its full height?

To start, click the "Start" button, set the lighting conditions, then click the "Grow!" button to make the plant start growing!
